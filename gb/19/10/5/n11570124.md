<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>丁海寅亲切台语问好 金钟新生代男星展帅气</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910050138402122-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韩星丁海寅于10月5日在台北出席“第54届电视金钟奖”彩排记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年10月05日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台北报导）韩星<a href="https://github.com/qddt69/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%81%E6%B5%B7%E5%AF%85.md">丁海寅</a>5日登台出席第54届金钟奖，身穿时尚全黑西装搭配俐落油头亮相，以台语亲切打招呼，全场尖叫声爆满。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11570149" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910050138292122.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11570149" title="“第54届电视金钟奖”韩星 丁海寅节目彩排" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910050138292122.jpg" alt="“第54届电视金钟奖”韩星 丁海寅节目彩排" width="451" b="658" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“第54届电视金钟奖”韩星 <a href="https://github.com/qddt69/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%81%E6%B5%B7%E5%AF%85.md">丁海寅</a>节目彩排记者会，于2019年10月5日在台北举行。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><a href="https://github.com/qddt69/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E8%BD%A9%E7%9D%BF.md">张轩睿</a>穿着深绿丝绒西装，古典绅风；<a href="https://github.com/qddt69/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E5%85%B4%E5%93%B2.md">周兴哲</a>将救生衣西装穿出型男时尚，期待未来能从歌手升格MV导演；<a href="https://github.com/qddt69/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E4%BB%A5%E8%B1%AA.md">刘以豪</a>身穿墨绿西装，衬托腼腆笑容，访问时还有热情粉丝丢掷礼物进场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11570155" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910050734121487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11570155" title="" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910050734121487.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="678" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/qddt69/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E5%85%B4%E5%93%B2.md">周兴哲</a>将救生衣西装穿出型男时尚。（台视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>获奖呼声高的<a href="https://github.com/qddt69/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E6%98%87%E8%B1%AA.md">温昇豪</a>，身穿经典端正的正式西装；金钟视帝吴慷仁一出场让众人惊呼认不出来，他为戏改变为黝黑肤色与光头造型，展现演员的敬业态度与可塑性。</p>
<p>林柏昇（KID）特别向军中告假力挺出席金钟典礼，与黄鸿升（小鬼）搭档走星光大道，他兴奋喊话：“再半年就要出来和大家见面了，现在正在努力减肥。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11570169" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910050742251487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11570169" title="" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910050742251487.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="680" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">林柏昇（KID）特别向军中告假参加金钟盛会，并说再半年就要出来和大家见面了。现在正在努力减肥。（三立提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11570172" style="width: 464px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910050746281487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11570172" title="" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910050746281487.jpg" alt="" width="464" b="696" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">黄鸿升（小鬼）曾经拿过“五丑”，今天要雪耻，希望今年可以拿“五帅”，造型走保守风。（三立提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黝黑肌肤的王绍伟，身穿迷彩西装率领团队骑重机霸气亮相，吸引全场目光，直呼：“有种将帅要出征的氛围！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11570167" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910050739361487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11570167" title="" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910050739361487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">王绍伟身穿迷彩西装率领团队骑重机霸气亮相。（三立提供）</figcaption></figure>
