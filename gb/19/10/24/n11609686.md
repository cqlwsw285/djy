<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>李千那新戏不畏霸凌 演出横跨15年的角色</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910240834021470-ss1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>华视全新戏剧《守着阳光守着你》媒体特映会10月24日在台北举行。 图左起为吴品洁、李千娜。（黄宗茂／大纪元） 
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年10月24日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台北报导）《<a href="https://github.com/brkypg2370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%88%E7%9D%80%E9%98%B3%E5%85%89%E5%AE%88%E7%9D%80%E4%BD%A0.md">守着阳光守着你</a>》拍全新4K版本，今天（24日）举办媒体特映会，<a href="https://github.com/brkypg2370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%8D%83%E9%82%A3.md">李千那</a>从18岁演到33岁又重返大学的熟女，戏里戏外的个性努力不懈、坚韧不拔，都有相似之处，她表示看到首集剧本要演出横跨15年的角色，就非常想挑战。<br />
<figure id="attachment_11609701" style="width: 336px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910240456412122-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910240456412122-ss1.jpg" alt="吴品洁、李千娜《守着阳光守着你》" width="450" b="599" class="size-full wp-image-11609701" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/brkypg2370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E5%93%81%E6%B4%81.md">吴品洁</a>、<a href="https://github.com/brkypg2370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%8D%83%E9%82%A3.md">李千那</a>出席媒体特映会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<figure id="attachment_11609703" style="width: 391px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910240456382122-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910240456382122-ss1.jpg" alt="吴品洁、李千娜" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11609703" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/brkypg2370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E5%93%81%E6%B4%81.md">吴品洁</a>、李千那出席媒体特映会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吴品洁演出自私心机女，跟方琦演出坏心姊妹花霸凌李千那，她们表示第一天开拍就要对李千那泼水，压力超大，看到其他演员在片场玩开心，还是快乐不起来，角色个性让她久久无法抽离。但李千那大方开导她们不要紧张，“就来霸凌我吧！”才让两人使出演技诠释霸凌戏。</p>
<p>《<a href="https://github.com/brkypg2370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%88%E7%9D%80%E9%98%B3%E5%85%89%E5%AE%88%E7%9D%80%E4%BD%A0.md">守着阳光守着你</a>》2019年创作全新剧本，由是元介及李千那担纲男女主角。旧版的设定是男主角对女主角的守候，新版则是所有角色不论是亲情、友情、爱情都互相守候彼此，并邀请1982年版本的演员龙隆、金士杰、顾宝明在新版中主演及客串。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11609704" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910240456502122-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910240456502122-ss1.jpg" alt="李千那" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11609704" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">李千那出席媒体特映会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11609705" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910240456532122-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910240456532122-ss1.jpg" alt="吴品洁" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11609705" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">吴品洁出席媒体特映会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11609707" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910240457012122-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910240457012122-ss1.jpg" alt="方琦" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11609707" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">方琦出席媒体特映会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><a href="https://github.com/brkypg2370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E5%A4%A7%E5%A4%A9.md">陈大天</a>在剧中与何紫妍（豆豆）是欢喜冤家，李千那爆料有次他自高奋勇要载豆豆到另一个拍摄地，竟疑似“故意”迷路，两人晚了40分钟才到拍摄现场，陈大天紧张的喊冤，“真的迷路到不知道怎么跟剧组人员解释”。</p>
<p>同组女演员则夸赞<a href="https://github.com/brkypg2370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E5%A4%A7%E5%A4%A9.md">陈大天</a>是大暖男，拍戏时相当照顾大家，在车上都会配给每个演员专属座位及送上矿泉水，只差没帮女生准备卫生棉了，陈大天笑说：“其实我有在兼职开UBER。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11609708" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910240456312122-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1910240456312122-ss1-600x456.jpg" alt="潘柏希、吴品洁、李千娜、陈大天《守着阳光守着你》" width="600" b="456" class="size-large wp-image-11609708" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（左起）潘柏希、吴品洁、李千娜、陈大天出席媒体特映会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
