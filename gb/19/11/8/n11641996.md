<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>和女友穿情侣装？ 邱泽藉比喻暗指自己单身</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911080732032384-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>邱泽资料照。（黄宗茂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年11月08日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台北报导）<a href="https://github.com/cepxz249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%82%B1%E6%B3%BD.md">邱泽</a>、<a href="https://github.com/cepxz249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%AD%E9%9B%AA%E8%8A%99.md">郭雪芙</a>与<a href="https://github.com/cepxz249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E5%BF%B5%E8%BD%A9.md">吴念轩</a>8日出席时尚品牌活动，三人近期都恰好传出恋情，被问到是否会想和另外一半穿情侣装？邱泽幽默回应：“总要先有主机，再来想卡带的问题吧！”意指目前感情状态仍单身。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11642322" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911080731452384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11642322" title="" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911080731452384-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">艺人<a href="https://github.com/cepxz249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%82%B1%E6%B3%BD.md">邱泽</a>（左）、<a href="https://github.com/cepxz249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%AD%E9%9B%AA%E8%8A%99.md">郭雪芙</a>（右）8日在台北出席时尚品牌活动。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>邱泽与张钧甯的感情状态仍是媒体关注焦点，被问及：“会不会想送钧甯一套西装？”他大方笑答：“不知道她穿不穿西装，但觉得她满适合的！”</p>
<p>而郭雪芙与邱泽2012年曾合作偶像剧《螺丝小姐要出嫁》，时隔7年再度公开合体亮相，两人交情显得不错。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11642330" style="width: 456px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911080731002384.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11642330" title="" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911080731002384.jpg" alt="" width="456" b="684" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">艺人邱泽8日在台北出席时尚品牌活动。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11642334" style="width: 454px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911080730132384.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11642334" title="" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911080730132384.jpg" alt="" width="454" b="681" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">艺人邱泽8日在台北出席时尚品牌活动。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下个月将迎接圣诞节，郭雪芙表示当天应该会在拍戏中度过，如果可以庆祝的话，希望能抽空与家人、朋友玩交换礼物，被问及是否会买礼物给传闻中的男友孙其君？她笑而不答。邱泽则表示，他在圣诞节隔日要演出舞台剧《暗恋桃花源》，所以节日当天应该是在忙排戏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11642331" style="width: 454px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911080731232384.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11642331" title="" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911080731232384.jpg" alt="" width="454" b="681" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">艺人郭雪芙8日在台北出席时尚品牌活动。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11642335" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911080730062384.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11642335" title="" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911080730062384.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">艺人郭雪芙8日在台北出席时尚品牌活动。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><a href="https://github.com/cepxz249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E5%BF%B5%E8%BD%A9.md">吴念轩</a>之前被拍到与大他2岁的吴心缇，在台北西门町的健身房约会，两人IG上除了互相追踪，也会为对方的发文送上爱心。对此，他笑说跟吴心缇只是朋友，偶尔互相关心，简单回答完随即离去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11642332" style="width: 457px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911080731112384.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11642332" title="" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911080731112384.jpg" alt="" width="457" b="686" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">艺人吴念轩8日出席时尚品牌活动。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11642336" style="width: 455px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911080729312384.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11642336" title="" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911080729312384.jpg" alt="" width="455" b="683" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">艺人吴念轩8日出席时尚品牌活动。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
