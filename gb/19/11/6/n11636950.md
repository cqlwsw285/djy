<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>黑糖珍珠奶浆蛋塔 流出的奶浆有如“土石流”</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/62164983_432390547559338_9107386711314268160_n-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“黑糖珍珠爆乳蛋塔”流出来的奶浆有如“土石流”。（吞吞手作提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年11月06日讯】（大纪元时报记者朱孝贞台北报导）台湾<a href="https://github.com/cepxz249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8F%8D%E7%8F%A0%E5%A5%B6%E8%8C%B6.md">珍珠奶茶</a>在日本爆红后，台湾也开始搞创新，开发出一系列的“<a href="https://github.com/cepxz249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8F%8D%E7%8F%A0.md">珍珠</a>系列美食”。想到珍珠奶茶的甜美滋味，让很多人都想尝尝新口味。你吃过哪几种珍珠口味的美食呢？</p>
<p>“<a href="https://github.com/cepxz249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8F%8D%E7%8F%A0.md">珍珠</a>系列美食”再一发，珍珠竟然被放进<a href="https://github.com/cepxz249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E5%A1%94.md">蛋塔</a>、披萨、<a href="https://github.com/cepxz249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E6%A3%92.md">冰棒</a>、泡芙等等。可以想像一下是什么滋味？这不算什么，还有高雄的店家更夸张，推出现做奶浆满溢的蛋塔。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11636997" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/S__9191436.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11636997" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/S__9191436-600x385.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="385" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/cepxz249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8F%8D%E7%8F%A0%E5%A5%B6%E8%8C%B6.md">珍珠奶茶</a><a href="https://github.com/cepxz249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E6%A3%92.md">冰棒</a>。（陈安／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11636996" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/S__9191435.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11636996" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/S__9191435-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">掺有珍珠的披萨。（陈安／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黑糖珍珠奶浆满溢<a href="https://github.com/cepxz249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E5%A1%94.md">蛋塔</a>，流出来的奶浆有如“奶浆土石流”一泄不可收拾，真的是刺激人的口腹之欲。但又觉得有点罪恶感，吃了“会不会增胖啊！”哈哈真是很矛盾！很多人可能会想，算了先吃再减肥吧！</p>
<p>除了奶浆满溢珍珠蛋塔，还有珍珠闪电泡芙、伯爵珍珠。颠覆你对千层蛋塔的味蕾感受，满满的黑糖珍珠和特调伯爵奶茶酱，香醇的茶香味溢满口中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11636991" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/20190620120817-9a3be3a7.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11636991" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/20190620120817-9a3be3a7-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">满满的黑糖珍珠和特调伯爵奶茶酱。（吞吞手作提供）</figcaption></figure>
