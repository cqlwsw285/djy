<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：脚印</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/11/1511020458252124-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>弗雷西特国家公园的白沙滩&#8211;友好海滩（Friendly Beach）（华苜∕大纪元） 
</h6>
<hr>
<p>走上海滩。</p>
<p>多么松软的海滩！走过去，一步一个脚印。脚印留在身后。</p>
<p>脚到哪里，脚印到哪里。</p>
<p>猛回头，脚印已杳，不禁黯然。</p>
<p>原来是海浪和海风的杰作。海浪奔涌向海滩，一波紧接着一波，海风吹抚向海滩，一阵紧接着一阵，带动群沙，把脚印填平。</p>
<p>海滩上的脚印，能存留多久？</p>
<p>不禁想起小时候，每当干燥时节，总爱把脚印密密地印在泥土路上；但能存留多久？几趟车辆经过，几阵风吹过，脚印已杳。</p>
<p>海滩上的脚印存留不久，别地方的脚印就能存留多久？渺渺宇宙，茫茫人海，又有几 样能存留不朽？</p>
<p> <strong>Footprint</strong>&emsp; Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>March onto the beach.</p>
<p>How soft the beach! March on, a footprint is a step! The footprint leaves behind the body.</p>
<p>Where the foot is where the footprint is.</p>
<p>Look back suddenly, the footprint is dim, I can’t help to be sadly.</p>
<p>It’s the great creature of the spray and the sea wind. Spray gushes onto the beach, a wave after a wave, the sea wind blows to the beach, a spell after a spell, carries all the sand, fills and levels up the foot print.</p>
<p>How long does the footprint on the beach can leave?</p>
<p>I can’t help to think of the childhood, when it’s the drought season, we often printed the footprints on the mud path densely; but how long can it leave? After some times the vehicle passes over, some spells of wind blows over, the footprint is vanished.</p>
<p>No long does the footprint left, how long does the footprint left on other place? Vague the universe, immerse the human sea, how many of them can leave forever still? @</p>
<p>责任编辑：林芳宇</p>
